GENERAL PEST MANAGEMENT
Pest covered:- Cockroaches, red ants, black ants, and silver fish.

   General first control management services provide protection against crawling insect pests such as cockroaches, red and black ants, and silverfish.






RODENT ABATEMENT
Pest covered:- Pest covered:- Rat, Mice etc.

   We will make available bait stations on demand to extrude the threats of rats and mites from your facility. The exterior perimeter of the facility shall be placed with steel or metal traps, and microencapsulated baits, etc to avoid the entry of the rodents into the facility.





FLY AND MOSQUITO MANAGEMENT

   Flies and mosquitoes are vectors of harmful pathogens. The treatment includes fogging, fumigation (ULV), residual spray, etc. Primordial Services ensures control of flies and mosquitoes on your premises.







ANTI-TERMITE TREATMENT
Pre-construction and Post-construction treatment on construction projects with guarantee

   Pre-construction termite proofing: When applied during the construction of the building, it protects the building from termite infestation for years.
Post-construction termite proofing: Eradication and prevention of termite infestation in already constructed buildings. Termiticide with the long residual action is injected through drill holes made at infested areas of the building, especially along the wall-floor joints.
