WHAT IS PEST CONTROL?

   Pest control is the process of minimising or removing a wide range of undesirable insects and other pests from spaces occupied by people. The actual process of pest control can take place in the home, in a place of business or in a public building. Controlling pests normally involves using some type of insecticides to prevent them from entering the space and kill any pests that have already established a presence within the space.



WHY IS PEST CONTROL IMPORTANT?

   Pest control is important because best can carry harmful bacteria that can contaminate foods and cause illness. These harmful bacteria can be passed to the food by contact with their hair feces and urine. Pests can also cause serious damage to the structure and fabric of food premises.

WHAT IS IPM?

   IPM stands for integrated Pest Management. Food, water, and shelter are conducive conditions for pests to thrive on. Our pest management professionals will find out about these micro inhabitants to eliminate the development of insects. IPM programs include cleaning, solid waste management, structural maintenance, customer education, and finally appropriate pesticide application. IPM entirely differs from the traditional past in this way. So Primordial Services promote safe, efficient, and environmentally sound strategies for the prevention and control of disease vectors and other pests that may adversely affect health, impede operations, and damage food and property.
